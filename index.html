MAYA Model

MAYA is a self-sustaining model built around the idea of bringing a local community together for local economic growth, employment generation, and improving EV Infrastructure of the country.

Our Components

EV Charger

EV Charger is a device that supplies electric energy to charge an electric vehicle's battery. This charge can be provided by plugging the charger into an electric vehicle or by connecting the charger to a charging station.

Cafe

The Cafe is a place where people can come together to enjoy a cup of coffee, tea, or other beverages. It is a place where people can relax, socialize, and enjoy the company of others.

Washroom

The Washroom is a facility that provides a place for people to use the restroom, wash their hands, and freshen up. It is an essential component of any public space.

Green Energy

Green Energy is a type of energy that is produced from renewable sources, such as wind, solar, and hydro power. It is a clean and sustainable form of energy that helps reduce greenhouse gas emissions and combat climate change.

Kiosk

The Kiosk is a small, standalone structure that provides information, products, or services to the public. It is a convenient and accessible way for people to access information and make purchases.
